About Us

Shri Yogindra Sagar Institute of Technology & Science (SYSITS) was established in 2008. The institute is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and is affiliated to RGPV, Bhopal. SYSITS is one of the premier institutes in the Malwa region, committed to providing quality education in the field of engineering and technology.

Our mission is to foster a culture of excellence and innovation, and to create an environment that nurtures intellectual and personal growth. We are dedicated to producing competent and skilled professionals who can contribute to the development of the nation.
